About

I'm a Homo sapiens born and raised in Hong Kong. I also spent a year studying in the UK and semesters in the US and France, experiences that opened my mind and shaped how I see the world.

Before university, I was that kid obsessed with biology and completely hooked on the Olympiad. I explored the mysteries of life. But after countless hours pipetting in the lab, I started to feel burnt out. I realized I loved biology, just not the endless wet lab work.

Some time, I discovered the beauty and speed of simulations, where you can explore systems without spilling a single drop. One day, I had a lightbulb moment: "What if I could model ecosystems and solve big biology questions instead?" That idea completely changed my path.

And so, here I am, merging my love for biology with the power of math and computation.
